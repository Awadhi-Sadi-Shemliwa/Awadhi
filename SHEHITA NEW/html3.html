OUR TEAM

Sadi Shemliwa

EXECUTIVE CHAIRMAN

Founder Director and Executive Chairman of SHEHITA, founder and Managing Director of Actuarial and Risk Consulting (T) Limited.

Over twenty-four (24) years of experience working as Consultant in Strategic Planning, Risk Management, and Actuarial Valuation in Insurance and Pensions. Advises Board and Management in Strategic Planning and Risk Management. Advises both trustees and sponsors of employee benefits schemes in the areas of scheme design, funding strategies and valuation, and investment policy statement and strategy. Also advises both life and general (re)insurance companies in the areas of pricing and reserving, reinsurance optimization, and capital management and capital adequacy.

Holds Master of Science Degree in Actuarial Management (MSc ActMgt) and Master of Science in Actuarial Science (MSc ActSci) from City University London; Master of Arts Degree in Economic Policy Management (MA-EPM) from Makerere University Kampala, and a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Statistics (BA Stat-Hons) from University of Dar es Salaam. Certified Enterprise Risk Manager (CERM) from Asia Risk Management Institute (ARiMI) and National University of Singapore (NUS).
