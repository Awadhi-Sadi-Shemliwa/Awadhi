Word From Managing Director

Awadhi Shedafa

MANAGING DIRECTOR

Since our establishment in 2006, when we commenced processing forest products at our plant in Mafinga, we have evolved into one of the most dynamic companies in our industry. We have passed milestones in our operations resulting in a tripling of our annual production capacity, exceeding all expectations.

The result-driven performance that we have achieved, together with our values of determination, integrity, and commitment has put us at the forefront of the Tanzania market and, more importantly, placed us in a prime position in the export market.

I am especially proud of the recognition and support of leading organizations and industry operators throughout East Africa and believe the prospects for SHEDA GENERAL SUPPLIES LTD's continued growth and development are excellent. In closing, I would like to thank all our employees, contractors, clients, and associates for their continued support and for being a part of our success.

Treatment

Sheda GS ensures that every transmission pole meets the highest standards of quality. From selection to final placement, the process is carried out meticulously to ensure durability and reliability in every product.

Sheda GS prides itself on maintaining the best practices in timber processing. By adhering to stringent guidelines, we deliver premium poles that are engineered for resilience and longevity.

Sheda GS guarantees that our timber undergoes rigorous inspections and treatments. Our commitment to quality means that every pole not only meets but exceeds industry standards for performance.

Checks and Transportation

Sheda GS always makes sure quality control checks and transportation of timber poles are done at the highest standards. We ensure that each pole is carefully inspected before being dispatched to its final destination.

Sheda GS uses advanced logistics for safe and timely delivery. Each pole is properly secured and handled, ensuring no damage during transport.

Sheda GS guarantees smooth delivery processes, adhering to safety protocols and delivering poles with a focus on efficiency and care.

Harvesting

Sheda GS follows sustainable harvesting methods that prioritize environmental conservation while ensuring high-quality timber poles. Our harvesting teams are trained to operate with care for natural resources.

Sheda GS is committed to sustainability, ensuring that all harvested timber meets strict environmental regulations while maintaining top quality.

Sheda GS guarantees responsible sourcing of raw materials, ensuring that our poles are produced in an eco-friendly manner, reducing our environmental footprint.

Transport

Sheda GS ensures that every aspect of transportation is handled with the utmost care. Our poles are securely loaded and shipped to customers efficiently, ensuring they arrive in perfect condition.

Sheda GS uses modern transportation methods and equipment to guarantee the safe and timely delivery of our timber poles, minimizing delays and ensuring client satisfaction.

Sheda GS ensures a seamless transport process, allowing clients to receive products that are ready for immediate use, without any delays or damages.
